# 📞 Call Center Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project delivers an interactive **Excel Dashboard** analyzing call center operations
and performance. It provides a comprehensive view of call volume trends, representative
performance, customer satisfaction, and purchase behavior — all filterable by call duration,
representative, and year.

---

## 📂 Dataset
- **Source:** Call Center Dataset (Excel)
- **Content:** Information on call volume, caller demographics, call duration,
representative performance, customer satisfaction scores, and average purchase values.

**Key metrics analyzed:**
- Total Calls & Gender Breakdown (Male / Female)
- Average Caller Age
- Monthly Call Volume Trends
- Representative Performance Scores
- Average Purchase Value by Call Duration
- Customer Satisfaction Scores by Call Duration

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall call center activity through Total, Male, and Female call KPIs.
- Analyze monthly call volume trends to identify peak and low periods.
- Evaluate individual representative performance scores across the team.
- Understand how call duration impacts average purchase value.
- Measure customer satisfaction levels across different call duration segments.
- Enable flexible filtering by duration, representative, and year for deeper insights.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Data Processing:** Power Query (Excel)
- **Visualization:** Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, PivotCharts, Slicers, KPI Cards,
  Line Chart, Bar Charts, Donut Chart)
- **Data Source:** Microsoft Excel (.xlsx)

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow
1. **Data Cleaning:** Standardizing call duration categories, representative IDs,
   and handling any missing demographic or satisfaction values using Power Query.
2. **Feature Engineering:** Grouping call durations into segments
   (Under 10 mins, 10–30 mins, 30–60 mins, 1–2 hours, More than 2 hours)
   and calculating average satisfaction and purchase values per segment.
3. **Dashboard Development:** Building an interactive single-page Excel dashboard with:
   - KPI cards (Total Calls, Male Calls, Female Calls, Average Age)
   - Line chart for monthly Call Volume Trend (Jan–Dec)
   - Bar chart for Representative Performance (R01–R05)
   - Bar chart for Average Purchase by Call Duration
   - Donut chart for Customer Satisfaction by Call Duration
   - Slicers for Call Duration, Representative, and Year (2023 / 2024)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

- **Call Volume:** A total of **1,000 calls** were recorded — **401 Male** and **599 Female**,
  with an average caller age of **33**.
- **Call Trend:** The line chart reveals noticeable fluctuations in monthly call volume,
  helping identify high-demand periods throughout the year.
- **Representative Performance:** Performance scores vary across representatives (R01–R05),
  making it easy to spot top performers and those needing support.
- **Purchase by Duration:** Longer calls tend to correlate with higher average purchase values,
  suggesting that more engaged conversations drive better outcomes.
- **Customer Satisfaction:** The donut chart shows how satisfaction levels differ across
  call duration segments, highlighting which durations lead to the best customer experience.

📌 **Key Insight:** Call duration is a critical factor influencing both **purchase value**
and **customer satisfaction**, making it a key metric for optimizing representative training
and call handling strategies.
